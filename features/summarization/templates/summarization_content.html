Create Executive PowerPoint

The PowerPoint generation AI Agent uses the same "Agent of Agents" approach as Text Summary to transform documents into compelling executive presentations.

The process involves a two-step embedded AI workflow:

Intelligent Content Analysis:

An AI Agent analyzes the core themes, structure, and key information within the uploaded document and creates a relevant and coherent outline.

Crafting Agents:

Based on the analysis a dedicated domain expert AI Agent crafts the slide content and structure and generates the final PowerPoint deck.

Together this results in a structured, contextually relevant PowerPoint.
