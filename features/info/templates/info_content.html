A Legacy of Impact & Innovation

Throughout his career, Paul has consistently driven revenue growth and competitive differentiation. At OpenText, he spearheaded the launch of new cloud services like Secure Mail and Secure MFT, achieving nearly 100% revenue growth for the Information Exchange Suite in just two years and earning the Field Leadership Award. He also led global product management for AI, Analytics, and Cyber Security portfolios, delivering $50M in annual revenue and launching the cloud-native AI product, Risk Guard.
Earlier, at IBM, Paul was instrumental in defining and bringing to market the solution now known as "IBM Watson Content Analytics," contributing to over $10M in new license revenue and supporting the architecture design for the groundbreaking IBM Watson Jeopardy project. His expertise spans diverse sectors including MLOps, Data Management, Business Intelligence, RPA, Content Management, and Cyber Security, consistently delivering successful SaaS and on-premise solutions.
