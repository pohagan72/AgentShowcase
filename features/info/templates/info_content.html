  • Driving Revenue Growth: I built and spearheaded a new sales division for a partner program, growing it from the ground up to $10M in annual revenue and managing a portfolio of 75 partners.
  • Launching Cloud-Native Products: I've defined and brought brand-new SaaS product to market, from the cloud-native AI product Risk Guard at OpenText to the AI Platform at Epiq Global for advanced eDiscovery.
  • Fostering Strategic Partnerships: I have a track record of building key alliances with industry leaders like Google Cloud, AWS, Azure, Chubb, and Salesforce to create differentiated, integrated solutions.
  • Enabling M&A Success: Owned the technical alignment planning for over $100,000,000 in acquisitions, including most recently the Epiq Global strategic acquisition of Laer which created a new AI-first eDiscovery product unit.

This showcase is the next chapter in that story. It's where I explore, build, and share my work as I reinvent myself as an "AI-Native" Product Manager, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
